Rich/Wealth

The 400 wealthiest Americans control $2.29 trillion in wealth. Very wealthy people tend to own many shares of stock, so the wealth of the very rich has recovered sharply from the recession and reached a new all-time high even as average incomes stagnate.The top 1% of America owns 50% of investment assets (stocks, bonds, mutual funds).The 1% also takes home 20% of the income. 97% of millionaires are also homeowners, and the average home of a millionaire is valued at $320,000.
